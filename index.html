Privacy Policy

Effective Date: March 7, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how information is collected, used, and protected when you interact with the Construction Timesheet Assistant voice and SMS messaging system.

Information We Collect

When you interact with this service, we may collect information including your phone number, SMS message content, call recordings or transcripts, timestamps, and interaction logs.

How Information Is Used

Information collected through this service is used solely to operate the messaging system, process work-log messages, and generate requested summaries.

Third-Party Services

This service uses Twilio, a third-party communications provider, to process SMS messages and voice calls.

SMS Messaging Compliance

SMS opt-in consent and phone numbers collected for SMS messaging will not be shared with any third parties or affiliates for marketing or promotional purposes.

Opt-Out

You may opt out of SMS communications at any time by replying STOP to any message.
